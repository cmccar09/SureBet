💡 Scoring Methodology

How the 14-Factor Model Works

Each horse is scored across 14 independent factors totalling up to ~150pts (no hard cap). Scores above 60 are considered confidence-grade. Each race's best-scoring horse is identified, then the top 3 cross-race bests become today's picks.

Factor Hierarchy (most to least impactful)

  • Going Suitability (up to 32pts): In Soft/Heavy ground, horses that have proven form in testing conditions earn double base points. Unproven horses (0 wins) in extreme going receive a penalty.
  • Recent Win (+22pts): Last-to-race winner is the single strongest predictor. Captures peak fitness and confidence.
  • Odds Sweet Spot (+12pts): 3-9 decimal range covers ~70% of UK winners. Graduated scale outside this range.
  • C/D Win Marker (+18pts): Explicit Course and Distance winner badge from racecard. Primary evidence of suitability.
  • Distance Suitability (+18pts): Proven at the target trip — uses C/D marker as primary evidence, form win count as proxy.
  • Trainer Tier (+4–15pts): 3-tier system from elite (Mullins/Henderson) to decent. Elite bonus capped lower than old flat bonus to avoid over-penalising weaker picks.
  • Jockey Quality (+6–12pts): 2-tier system from champion joskeys to very good jockeys. Claiming jockeys also rewarded in soft going (+allowance × 2).
  • NEW — Jockey Course Familiarity (+4–8pts): DynamoDB tracks wins/runs per jockey per course. ≥30% win rate = full bonus. Grows over time as results are recorded.
  • NEW — Relative Weight (+/-8pts): Weight within field context: 10+ lbs lighter than average = advantage. 10+ lbs heavier = burden. Requires Betfair RUNNER_METADATA weight data.
  • NEW — Official Rating Bonus (+4–8pts): OR ≥155 = championship class (+8pts). OR ≥140 = Group/Grade 2-3 class (+4pts).
  • Market Leader (+12pts): Race favourite aggregates all public information — when AI agrees with market, confidence is reinforced.
  • Form Wins (+8pts each) & Consistency (+4pts each place): Raised from 5/2 to reward horses with evidence of winning.
  • Going Penalty (up to −32pts): Zero wins in extreme going = full base penalty. Removed for Graded races (elite horses are trained for all conditions).
  • AW Low Class Penalty (−35pts): Class 5/6 AW handicaps are unpredictable by design. System aggressively avoids these.

Self-Learning System

After each day's racing completes, daily_learning.py fetches settled results from Betfair, compares our picks to actual winners, and nudges factor weights in DynamoDB (±0.3–1.5pts). Over time this adapts the model to recent track/going biases. The system also now records jockey-course combinations (wins/runs) which will power the familiarity bonus in future scans.

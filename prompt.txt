**PRIORITY Factors (in order):**

1. **VALUE EDGE (CRITICAL - HARD RULE)** - Market efficiency check
   - **NEVER BET IF edge_percentage < 0%** = AUTOMATIC REJECTION
   - Negative edge means market knows something we don't

2. **VALUE & FORM** - Best combination of value + recent winning form
   - Odds 3.0-9.0 with recent win = IDEAL target
   - Odds 2.5-2.9 or 9.0-12.0 with exceptional form = Consider
   - Focus on WINNERS, not just placers

3. **CLASS CONTEXT (CRITICAL NEW RULE)** - Wins must be in relevant class
   - **Recent win in SAME class** > multiple wins in lower class
   - **Class step-up risk:** If last wins were in lower class, reduce confidence by 30%
   - **Class drop opportunity:** Won at higher class before, increase confidence by 20%
   - **ALWAYS verify class level of last 3 wins** - context matters more than count
   - Form from 2 classes below = largely irrelevant

4. **COURSE FORM (MAJOR EDGE)** - Proven winners at this specific track
   - **Course winner (1+ wins here):** +25 confidence points (proven edge)
   - Course placed (2-3 finishes): +12 points
   - Never raced here: Neutral (not negative, but needs strong form)
   - **Course winners repeat at ~25% higher rate** than first-timers
   - Course form > generic form in close calls

5. **RECENT WINNER** - Must have WON (not just placed) in last 3-5 races
   - Win in last race **in same/higher class**: +30 confidence points
   - Win in last 2-3 races **in same/higher class**: +20 confidence points
   - Multiple wins in last 5 **in same/higher class**: +40 confidence points
   - **Wins in lower class:** Half points (e.g., +15 instead of +30)

6. **GOING MATCH PRECISION (NEW WEIGHTING)** - Track conditions matter
   - **"Perfect" going match:** +15 confidence points (rare, valuable edge)
   - "Good" going match: +5 confidence points (common)
   - "Unknown" going match: -10 confidence points (hidden risk)
   - Perfect conditions can make 15-20% performance difference

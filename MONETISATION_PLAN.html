⚠️ Legal Requirements (UK)

What you must know before charging for tips

  • Gambling Commission licence: You do NOT need a licence to provide tips for free, but providing tips as a commercial service (charging money) requires a Remote Betting Intermediary licence under the Gambling Act 2005.
  • Tipster exemption: Many UK tipsters operate under the "information service" framing — providing data/analysis rather than a betting service. Legal advice is strongly recommended before launching a paid service.
  • Affiliate links: Entirely legal — no licence required. Regulated under ASA/CAP advertising rules (must disclose affiliate relationships).
  • Terms & Conditions: Must include "for entertainment purposes only", "past performance does not guarantee future results", and problem gambling links (GambleAware).
  • Age verification: Must prevent under-18s from accessing paid content.
  • Start here: Gambling Commission website (gamblingcommission.gov.uk) → "Do I need a licence?"
